口语表达

连接词

口语没有时间去查单词，需要相当及时的表达出来，那就用一些连接词给自己一点缓冲时间

比如：

  • 副词开头：Commonly, Frankly
  • 缓冲句：That’s good question，You know，It’s hard to say
  • 连接词：so that, then,well

换一种说法

有时候会卡在某个单词，换一种说法口语讲明白即可

  • 蛋黄：the yellow part of the egg
  • 举例子：For example, xxx

闲聊

周边

  • Do you like the weather/food in Hangzhou？
  • How was your trip？

问候

  • Hi buddy, How are you? Hope you had a good weekend :)

文化

  • What’s your popular food in your country ?

会议

开始

人物介绍

  • Hi xxx, Nice to meet you,This is PD xx, This is UED xx, This is team leader of XR project xx

  • My spoken English is not good, so I invite the xx to join us

讨论话题

So, Let’s disccus the topics mentioned in the email:

1.xxx

2.xxx

3.xxx

cue人

  • xxx, Could you introduce the current research of your team to us?

  • xxx, Could you explain the ihome product demand for AR to us?

确认计划

  • How long do you guys need to do this？

等人

  • Sorry, we need to wait for him, he may be dealing with some urgent matters

确认意图

###整体

  • Sorry,I don’t understand the meaning of xxx
  • Sorry,what did you mean with xxx
  • Pardon?

###词汇

  • Sorry,I don’t understand the item xxx, Could you explain this?

结束

  • Ok,It seems we have made some progress，Thank you all !
  • Do you have any questions to us？

专业词

要准确表达含义，一些常用专业词还是要记住，比如IT行业的：Distributed Storage System,Seller,Concurrency,etc

常用语法

被动

  • It looks like the callback is called from didRemoveAnchors and not from didUpdateAnchors

第三人称

  • MediaPipe has made it extremely easy to build our 3D person pose reconstruction demo app
  • He has a very detailed breakdown, he will be sharing it soon
  • it depends on how much resrouces they can provide

时态

一般现在时

  • Since their rendering engine does not include all the capabilities of our original rendered, we need to develop “workarounds”.

一般过去时

  • Sagi’s child has become sick and he had to leave to take him to the hospital ):

正在进行时

  • We are trying to complete this without him, but we don’t have anyone as proficient as him in this environment

过去完成时

  • The code had been summitted

一般将来时

  • the main benefit of the process will be gained with the cloud rendering

现在完成时

  • I’ve created the offline rendering for the POC and I’m responsilbe for replacing it with the T3D server rendering cluster

定语从句

  • I’m now working on the lighting which is the last problematic part
  • Maybe it is actually better to do it in a place which is not a meeting room?

常用语

观点

赞同

  • Agree
  • That explains it
  • Interesting
  • No wonder!
  • There we go!

有异议

  • I‘m really appreciate what you have done.But xxx
  • Are you sure it is realistic? Does it include enough time for end-to-end integration

稍后

  • I will get back to you after xxx
  • That’s a good question. I’ll xxx
  • I’ll take a better look later today
  • We will review later today

态度

###请求

  • Can you help me check this?
  • Is it possible for you to xxx?
  • I’d like to xxx
  • Let me know what you think / If you have any suggestion let me know

###赞赏

  • Amazing, Thanks for the quick response!
  • Thanks for the work
  • It’s a pleasure working together. A good start

###解释

  • Let me explain what I ment
  • Let me explain why this happened

###建议

  • I suggest that / My suggestion is

原谅

  • No worries, I took that into consideration when I hired him
  • It ‘s ok

谦虚

  • Anytime
  • My pleasure
